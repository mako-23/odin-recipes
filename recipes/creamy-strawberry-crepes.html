Creamy Strawberry Crepes

Home

This strawberry crepe recipe has been a family favorite for over 30 years! These crepes are delicious and very rich! Be sure you have at least 1 hour to prepare them; they are worth every minute!

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Ingredients to make creamy strawberry crepes
  3. Place flour, milk, water, eggs, melted butter, and salt into a blender; blend until smooth and set aside.
  4. A blender with blended crepe batter
  5. Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  6. A glass bowl with confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla being beaten together with a handmixer
  7. Gently fold in whipped cream.
  8. A large bowl of cream cheese-whipped cream filling
  9. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 2 tablespoons for each crepe.
  10. A greased pan with crepe batter
  11. Tip and rotate the pan to spread batter as thinly as possible.
  12. A greased pan with a thin layer of crepe batter
  13. Flip over when batter is set and edges begin to brown. Continue cooking until bottom begins to brown. Stack finished crepes on a plate; cover with a damp towel.
  14. A greased pan with a golden brown crepe
  15. Fill each crepe with 1/4 cup sliced strawberries and 1/3 cup cream cheese filling.
  16. A cutting board with cooked, rolled up crepes, with an open crepe filled with cream and sliced strawberries
  17. Roll up and top with a dollop of cream cheese filling and more sliced strawberries.
  18. A plate of creamy strawberry crepes topped with whipped cream, sliced strawberries, and powdered sugar
